Shipping Policy

EasyBuyOnline

Order Processing Time

All orders placed through EasyBuyOnline are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation. Processing includes order verification, packaging, and handover to the shipping carrier. Orders placed on weekends or United States federal holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Shipping Coverage

EasyBuyOnline ships exclusively within the continental United States. Shipping services are not available to addresses outside the United States, including Alaska, Hawaii, United States territories, or international destinations.

Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery time for shipments within the United States is 7 to 14 business days from the date of order processing. Business days are defined as Monday through Friday, excluding United States federal holidays. Delivery timeframes are estimates provided by shipping carriers and are subject to factors beyond the store's control, including carrier delays, weather events, and customs procedures for international border crossings within carrier networks.

Shipping Costs

The final price displayed for each product on the EasyBuyOnline website includes all applicable costs. This price is fully inclusive of the product cost, any applicable taxes, and standard shipping charges to addresses within the continental United States. Customers are not required to pay any additional fees, surcharges, or hidden costs beyond the price shown on the product page at the time of purchase.

Tracking Information

Every shipment dispatched from EasyBuyOnline includes a tracking number. This tracking number is automatically sent to the email address provided by the customer during checkout once the order has been processed and handed to the shipping carrier. Customers can use this tracking number on the carrier's official website to monitor the progress of their delivery.

Delayed Shipments and Compensation

If a shipment exceeds 15 business days for delivery from the date of order processing, EasyBuyOnline provides compensation. The compensation amount is one United States dollar ($1.00) for each full business day of delay beyond the 15th business day. Compensation is issued as a store credit via the original payment method. To initiate a delay claim, the customer must contact the store through the official contact channel after the 15th business day has passed, providing the order reference number.

Incorrect Address Policy

EasyBuyOnline ships orders strictly to the shipping address provided by the customer during the checkout process. The store is not responsible for delivery failures, delays, or non-delivery resulting from an incorrect, incomplete, or unverifiable address submitted by the customer. It is the customer's responsibility to ensure the shipping address is accurate. In cases where a package is undeliverable due to an address error, the product price may be refunded according to the terms outlined in the store's Refund & Return Policy. Shipping costs are non-refundable in such instances.

Order Modifications and Cancellations

Orders cannot be modified, altered, or canceled after the payment process has been successfully completed. Customers are advised to review their order details, including selected items and shipping address, carefully before finalizing payment. All sales are final upon payment confirmation.
