Privacy Policy

📄Introduction

EasyBuyOnline is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our services. We respect your privacy and are dedicated to handling your personal data with transparency and care.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data. By using our website, you acknowledge you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

📋Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact customer service. We do not collect unnecessary sensitive data.

The categories of personal information we collect include:

  • Identifiers: Your full name and email address.
  • Contact Details: Your phone number, billing address, and shipping address.
  • Commercial Information: Records of products or services purchased, obtained, or considered, including order-related information, purchase history, and preferences.
  • Correspondence: The content of your communications with our customer support team.

⚙️How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for various business purposes, including:

  • Order Processing & Fulfillment: To process your transactions, deliver products, and provide the services you request.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and manage your account.
  • Shipping Coordination: To coordinate with logistics partners for the delivery of your orders.
  • Website Improvement: To analyze site usage, improve our website's functionality and user experience, and develop new products and services.
  • Marketing Communications: To send you promotional messages, newsletters, and special offers only with your explicit, prior consent. You may opt-out of receiving these communications at any time.

💳Payment Processing

All financial transactions on the EasyBuyOnline platform are securely processed through our trusted, PCI-DSS compliant payment service provider, Skrill.

Important Security Notice: EasyBuyOnline does not store your full card numbers, bank account details, or any other payment credentials on our servers. All payment information is transmitted directly and securely to Skrill for processing. We only receive and store transaction confirmation details necessary for order fulfillment and customer service.

🤝Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing purposes. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share information with trusted third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as Skrill for payment processing and shipping carriers (e.g., FedEx, DHL, national postal services) to fulfill and deliver your orders. These partners are contractually bound to use your information only for the specific service provided and to protect it in accordance with this policy.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, court order, or similar legal process.

🔒Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of your personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption: Using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Secure Servers: Hosting our website and data on secure servers with robust firewall protection.
  • Restricted Access: Limiting access to your personal information to authorized employees, agents, and contractors who have a legitimate business need to know such information. They are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Ongoing Protection: Regularly reviewing and updating our security practices to address new threats and vulnerabilities.

🍪Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand how our website is used. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help the site remember your preferences and actions.

We use cookies for essential functions (like keeping items in your shopping cart) and for analytics to improve our site's performance. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

✓Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Rectification: To request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): To request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: To withdraw your consent for marketing communications or other processing activities based on consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Information" section below. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law. For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
