Refund & Return Policy

EasyBuyOnline

Return Eligibility

A product purchased from EasyBuyOnline may be eligible for return under the following conditions:

  • The product received does not match the image or description presented on the product page.
  • The product is broken, defective, or unusable upon arrival.
  • The product is in its original condition and packaging as specified in the Product Condition Requirements section.

Returns are accepted for products that are not as described, even if they are not technically defective. A product that matches its listing description and functions correctly is not eligible for return.

Return Timeframe

The customer must initiate a return request within one calendar day (24 hours) of receiving the product. The request is initiated by contacting the store via the designated professional email address. The return request timestamp is based on the time the contact email is received by the store. Requests received after this 24-hour period will not be accepted.

Return Shipping Costs

The customer is responsible for all costs associated with returning the product to EasyBuyOnline. This includes shipping fees, packaging materials, and any applicable insurance. The original shipping cost paid by the store to deliver the product to the customer is non-refundable. Refunds are calculated based on the product price only.

Product Condition Requirements

To qualify for a refund, the returned product must meet all of the following condition requirements:

  • The product must be in its original, undamaged packaging.
  • The product must be in its original, unused, and unaltered condition.
  • The product must not show damage caused by the customer beyond reasonable inspection and functional testing.
  • Minor opening of packaging to verify product existence, type, and basic functionality is permitted and will not void return eligibility.
  • Products that are broken or unusable must still be returned, unless otherwise instructed by the store. The store reserves the right to retain damaged products upon issuing a refund.

Refund Process

The refund process is initiated upon the store's receipt and inspection of the returned product. The process is as follows:

  • The customer must contact the store via the professional email address to request a return authorization.
  • Upon approval, the customer ships the product back to the address provided by the store.
  • Once the returned product is received and inspected, the store will notify the customer of the inspection results.
  • If the return is approved, a refund for the product price will be processed. The refund is issued directly back to the customer's original payment method via communication with the store's email address.
  • The time for the refund credit to appear in the customer's account depends on the processing times of the payment provider.

Exceptions

Certain situations are not eligible for return or refund under this policy:

  • Products damaged due to customer misuse, improper handling, neglect, or accidents after delivery.
  • Products that have been altered, modified, or repaired by anyone other than authorized personnel.
  • Products missing serial numbers, tags, or original packaging components.
  • Returns initiated after the 24-hour return window has closed.
  • Change of mind or incorrect order placement by the customer.
  • Products purchased during promotional sales or final clearance, unless otherwise specified.
