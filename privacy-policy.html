Privacy Policy

EasyBuyOnline

Information Collected

EasyBuyOnline collects information that is provided voluntarily and directly by customers. This information is necessary to process orders, facilitate shipping, and provide customer support. The specific data points collected include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address

This information is collected exclusively through the checkout process on the website or via direct contact forms. No other methods of data collection are employed.

Use of Information

The information collected by the store is used solely for the following operational purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling customer orders for physical kitchen products.
  • Arranging shipment of products within the United States.
  • Communicating order confirmations, shipping updates, and transactional information.
  • Responding to customer inquiries submitted via the store's contact methods.

The collected information is not used for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core transactional function of the store.

Payment Processing

EasyBuyOnline does not directly process or store payment card information. All payments are processed exclusively through the third-party payment service provider, Skrill. Customers are directed to Skrill's secure payment page to complete transactions. The store only receives confirmation of payment success or failure and does not have access to customers' full financial details during this process.

Data Storage and Security

Customer data is stored electronically within the store's website and hosting environment. The store implements standard technical measures designed to protect the security of this data against unauthorized access or disclosure. Data is not manually downloaded, transferred to local devices, or stored in physical formats by the store owner. Access to the administrative backend of the website is secured with strong credentials.

Data Sharing Limitations

Customer data is not sold, rented, or traded to any third parties. Data sharing is strictly limited to what is operationally required:

  • Skrill: Necessary transaction information is shared with Skrill to process payments, as governed by Skrill's own privacy policy.
  • Shipping Carriers: Name, phone number, and shipping address are shared with selected shipping carriers (e.g., USPS, UPS, FedEx) solely for the purpose of delivering purchased products.

No third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, tracking pixels, or marketing tools are used by the store.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in operational practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the store's services after any such changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

Contact Information

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or privacy practices, contact should be made using the professional email address associated with the EasyBuyOnline domain, available on the "Contact" page of the website. All communication regarding privacy matters will be handled through this channel.
